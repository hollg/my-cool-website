Our Services

Procurement and Acquisition Services

DAS provides centralized procurement services for federal agencies, ensuring compliance with Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) and maximizing cost efficiency through bulk purchasing agreements and strategic vendor partnerships.

Facilities Management

Our facilities management division oversees the maintenance, security, and operational efficiency of federal buildings and properties. Services include custodial operations, building maintenance, space planning, and energy management.

Human Resources Support

We offer comprehensive HR coordination services including position classification, salary administration, benefits management, and workforce development programs in accordance with Office of Personnel Management (OPM) guidelines.

Inter-Agency Coordination

The Inter-Agency Liaison Office facilitates communication and collaboration between federal departments, managing shared resources, coordinating joint initiatives, and maintaining government-wide administrative standards.

Policy Development and Compliance

Our policy division develops and maintains administrative procedures, ensures regulatory compliance, conducts internal audits, and provides guidance on federal administrative requirements.

Information Management Services

We maintain secure data systems for administrative records, provide document management solutions, and ensure compliance with federal records retention schedules and Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requirements.

Training and Development

DAS offers training programs for federal employees in administrative procedures, ethics compliance, procurement regulations, and management best practices.
