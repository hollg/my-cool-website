Welcome to the Department of Administrative Services

The Department of Administrative Services (DAS) is committed to providing efficient and effective support services to all federal agencies. Our mission is to streamline administrative processes, ensure regulatory compliance, and maintain the highest standards of public service.

Established in 1947, DAS has been at the forefront of administrative excellence, providing critical support functions including procurement, facilities management, human resources coordination, and inter-agency liaison services. We pride ourselves on our dedication to transparency, accountability, and fiscal responsibility.

Our staff of dedicated civil servants work tirelessly to ensure that government operations run smoothly and efficiently. Through innovative approaches to administrative challenges and a commitment to continuous improvement, we strive to serve the American people with integrity and professionalism.

For inquiries regarding administrative services, please contact your agency liaison officer or submit a request through the appropriate channels outlined in the Federal Administrative Procedures Manual (FAPM-2024).
